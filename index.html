Searching

Simple Search is connected with popular sale websites Ebay, Amazon, and Craigslist to make an easy all in one search.

Your Tracking List

Find what you are looking for but want to wait for a lower price? Add that item to your tracking list which is provided by the usage of React Native AsyncStorage.

Test it out!

Click on the phone screen on the left to test out Simple Search. Immediately get to searching for anything you have in mind! Save the item to your list or check out the item on the actual site.
